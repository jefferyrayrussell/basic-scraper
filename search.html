Search instructions

Public Health - Seattle & King County's Food Protection Program promotes healthy people and healthy communities through education and regulation of food service establishments. This website helps to educate the public about food establishment inspections in King County.

Search instructions:
  1. Enter business name, address, city and zip code, or any combination of the above.
  2. Select Inspection Type and Date Range, and other parameters. If you are not sure, just leave it blank.
  3. Click the "Search" button.

Note: If your search contains too many inspections, you may receive an error message. If that happens, return to this page and enter a narrower set of parameters.
